Fried Rice

Description

A simple fried rice with vegetables that can be made in 25 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp canola oil, divide
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • salt & ground pepper to taste
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 scallions, minced
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Wok on high heat, scramble eggs till set, transfer to plate and clean wok.
  2. Mix tblsp canola oil, carrots, and salt in wok, stir 2 minutes. Add garlic, cook till fragrant and browned. Stir peas and scallions in, cook till peas warmed. Lower heat to medium.
  3. Stir rice in; adds eggs back in, add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until heated through.
