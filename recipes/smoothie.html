Versatile Smoothie

Description

Build your own smoothie with a combination of any of these ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Kale
  • Any kind of berries
  • coconut water or plant based beverages
  • Any kind of fruit
  • Nut butter of your choice

Steps

  1. Add your ingredients into a blender and blend.
  2. Feel free to pick and choose based on your dietary restrictions and taste.
